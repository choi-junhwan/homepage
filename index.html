I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Astronomy department at University of Texas at Austin. My research focuses on galaxy formation and evolution and astrophysical dynamics, and tries to answer what the fundamental principles are in astronomical phenomena and how these principles are applied to astronomical problems. I received a Ph.D. in Astronomy from the Astronomy Department at University of Massachusetts at Amherst, with a thesis focused on dynamics of satellite and halo interaction.
